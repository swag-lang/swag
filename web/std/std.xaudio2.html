 [src]

LowPassFilter
BandPassFilter

Attenuates frequencies above the cutoff frequency (state-variable filter).
HighPassFilter

Attenuates frequencies outside a given range (state-variable filter).
NotchFilter

Attenuates frequencies below the cutoff frequency (state-variable filter).
LowPassOnePoleFilter

Attenuates frequencies inside a given range (state-variable filter).
HighPassOnePoleFilter

Attenuates frequencies above the cutoff frequency (one-pole filter, XAUDIO2_FILTER_PARAMETERS.OneOverQ has no effect) Attenuates frequencies below the cutoff frequency (one-pole filter, XAUDIO2_FILTER_PARAMETERS.OneOverQ has no effect).
